A Century of Dedication

Since 1905, Shri Nagpur Gujarati Mandal has been a shining beacon of knowledge. The Mandal began its journey with the founding of ‘Itwari Gujarati Shikshan Sanstha’. What started with just 28 pupils became a remarkable success, inspiring the Mandal to establish more schools over the next 100 years.

Today, the Mandal imparts knowledge to over 5,000 students across a variety of educational systems, including Hindi, Marathi, and English mediums. Despite facing its fair share of challenges and difficulties over the years, the Mandal has never wavered in its strive to provide the best possible education.

What began as a single primary school has grown into an impressive educational empire, thanks to the leadership of the early members who set the standard for transparency and responsibility. Their magnificent work has earned the lasting trust of the community.

Achievements & Recognition

Central School Status

The Department of Education granted the prestigious status of a Central School to Shri Umiyashankar Narayanji High School, recognizing its role as a pivotal educational hub.

NAAC Accreditation

The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) awarded B-Grade status to our college wing (VMV Commerce, JMT Arts, and JJP Science College) located in Wardhaman Nagar.

"The students of our institutions have consistently earned recognition for outstanding achievements in HSSC and University examinations, as well as representing their schools at district and state levels in athletics."

A Legacy of Trust

It is with great pleasure that the Mandal can proudly state that in the past 100 years, our institutions have successfully educated and positively impacted thousands of students, teachers, parents, and citizens. The remarkable success of our alumni is a testament to the Mandal’s commitment to mass education, literacy, and overall well-being.
